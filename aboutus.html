About Us

About Sadak Saathi

Sadak Saathi is a government-initiated digital platform designed to modernize how road infrastructure issues are reported and resolved across India. Our goal is to eliminate the delays, inefficiencies, and lack of trust that have historically plagued road complaint systems. With Sadak Saathi, citizens are empowered to report road obstructions, potholes, broken drainage systems, and other hazards through a transparent and accountable digital process.

Our Solution

We have built an end-to-end infrastructure complaint system that streamlines the entire journey from issue reporting to repair verification. Through a geo-tagged mobile app, users can raise complaints in real-time. These reports are then assigned to verified government contractors. Once work is completed, a crowd-verification process enables the public to validate the repair, ensuring transparency and trust at every stage.

Mission & Vision

Sadak Saathi exists to make Indian roads safer, smarter, and more citizen-driven. We aim to empower users of all literacy levels with simple tools, supported by video tutorials and an intuitive interface. Our AI-powered backend helps prioritize serious complaints, enabling faster resolutions and better allocation of municipal resources. Ultimately, we envision a system where no road complaint goes unheard, and every resolved issue leads to a safer journey for all.

What We Have Achieved

Since launch, Sadak Saathi has helped municipalities close hundreds of road-related complaints efficiently and with public oversight. Our platform has already seen wide adoption in pilot regions, reducing the time taken to address complaints and restoring citizens' faith in local governance. The visible complaint tracking, verified contractor work, and community involvement have created a trustworthy ecosystem around road safety.

Looking Ahead

All core features of the platform are live — including the mobile app, real-time complaint tracking, municipal integration, and AI prioritization. Our next milestone is to introduce a community rewards system that motivates civic participation by recognizing active contributors. With this, we aim to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that encourages proactive reporting and collective problem-solving.

This platform was conceptualized and built by a dedicated team — Abhist Kamle, Vedansh, Kunal, and Suraj — who bring their expertise in backend, machine learning, front-end development, and UI/UX design to deliver a reliable, citizen-first platform.

Join the Movement

Sadak Saathi isn’t just a digital tool — it’s a mission to make public infrastructure responsive, inclusive, and safe. Whether you're a daily commuter, a vigilant citizen, or a government body, Sadak Saathi invites you to be a part of real change. Let’s build roads we can trust — together.
